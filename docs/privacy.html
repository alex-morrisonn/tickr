Privacy Policy

Tickr is designed to be lightweight. It does not require an account, stores your preferences on device, uses local notifications if you enable them, and fetches calendar data so the app can show upcoming events.

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Privacy Terms Support

What Tickr Stores

Tickr stores settings such as watched pairs, filters, appearance preferences, and notification options on your device. Tickr does not require account creation or profile information to use the app.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Tickr uses local notifications on your device for event and session reminders. Notification access can be changed at any time in iOS Settings.

Calendar Data

Tickr fetches calendar data from a remote feed and may cache that data locally to improve load time and reliability. This is intended to provide current event data in the app.

Tracking

Tickr does not use third-party advertising SDKs and is not intended to track you across apps or websites.

Sharing

Tickr does not sell your personal information and is not intended to share personal data with advertising networks, data brokers, or third-party analytics providers.

Retention and Deletion

Preferences and cached calendar data remain on your device until you clear them in the app where available or delete the app from your device.

Questions

If you have privacy-related questions or want to request deletion of any information held by the app on your device, contact feedback@tickr.app.
