Tickr Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Tickr is an economic calendar app designed to help traders track scheduled market events and manage reminders.

Information Tickr Uses

Tickr stores app preferences on your device, such as watched pairs, filter settings, display preferences, and notification settings.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Tickr uses local notifications on your device to remind you about calendar events and market sessions. Tickr does not require an account to use these reminders.

Calendar Data

Tickr fetches calendar data from a remote JSON feed so the app can display upcoming economic events. This data is cached locally on your device to improve reliability and performance.

Analytics and Tracking

Tickr does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Data Sharing

Tickr does not sell your personal information. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated before those changes take effect.

Data Retention

Preferences and cached calendar data remain on your device until you remove the app or clear the app’s stored data where applicable.

Contact

If you have privacy-related questions, contact feedback@tickr.app.
